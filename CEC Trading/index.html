About Us

CEC Trading Ltd is a UK based sourcing organization and a trading company with over 4 years of experience in purchasing and sourcing in Asia. With our determination and quest for excellence, we have evolved in electronics and IT products importation and exportation, software development and cloud computing services over the years, gradually adjusting our business model from a trading company to a sourcing service and solution provider for overseas customers. Our success is contributed to its ability to understand the customer’s requirement and need. We strive to bring the best solution to every single client of ours. CEC Trading commits to provide the innovative products for the right customers at the right time through its professional services, such as manufacturer sourcing, product sourcing, product R&D, quality control measures, project management and logistics service in HK, mainland China, Taiwan, Singapore. Till now, we are proud of that we have attained a high reputation for our service quality and customer satisfaction.
